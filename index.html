What does your vote REALLY mean?
Voting is the cornerstone within the foundation of a strong, healthy democracy. Millions of people have given their lives to protect and advance democracy. One person, one vote... it is that simple and it is important.
Your voice matters and it must be counted or there is no true democracy.

For most of us living in modern democracies around the world this is an uncertainty.
The importance of each person having the ability to weigh in on issues that impact them, has been lost in a maze of voter fraud. We find ourselves pitted against eachother and facing competing political agendas that can creep all the way into the process itself, thereby resulting in corruption.

We have all seen the headlines of election tampering and questionable voting results. Research has even been published that documents the ease of hacking current electronic voting machine technology . So knowing the reality of current elections today how can we call any of our governments democracies? The truth is we can't. At least not yet.

Our Area of Work

What We Do

  • VeriCount Electoral Accountability System TM

    The system exposes and deters fraud with multiple third-party audits of official results. The simple-to-use phone and tablet apps allow activists, poll watchers, exit surveyors, election observers, and voters to develop duplicate data and verify official results at every stage of the electoral process, and to report on their findings almost instantly and with a high degree of accuracy.

  • Poll Watcher app and Exit Voting app

    The Poll Watcher app allows poll watchers to duplicate all the tallies collected by official poll workers, creating an independent record of precinct results and thus revealing any post-polling tampering. The Exit Voting app allows voters, upon departing their polling places, to re-record their votes, providing a 1:1 parallel count of the votes cast.
