About US

Welcome to Democracy COUNTS

It is our mission to defend the vote and empower citizens to hold their goverments accountable. Free and fair elections are a core principle of democracy. They hold leaders accountable and are the first step in the establishment of trust in government and law.

The VeriCount Electoral Accountability SystemTM apps empower citizens to watchdog their elections and hold officials to account when fraud occurs. The system exposes and deters fraud with multiple third-party audits of official results. The simple-to-use phone and tablet apps allow activists, poll watchers, exit surveyors, election observers, and voters to develop duplicate data and verify official results at every stage of the electoral process, and to report on their findings almost instantly and with a high degree of accuracy.
